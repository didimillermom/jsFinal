Overview

Site Purpose

This website is being created to showcase the skills that I have gained at BYUI in the Web Design/Development program. This is my Senior project.

Target Audience

The target audience will be potential employers ages30-60. Business owners who have positions available in front-end Web development or Design. The site will appeal to a mix of men and women in business. They will be accessing my site through various devices, including computers, laptops, and mobile devices.

Typography

For the typography, I wanted to keep the modern look and went with a sans serif and a more classic serif font as a contrast. The sans serif is "Raleway" and the serif font is "Palitino Linotype"
